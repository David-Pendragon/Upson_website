At Upson we are dedicated to providing the best possible service to our customers. We take pride in each product made by Epsom and if for any reason you are unhappy with a printer, let us know so we can make sure to fix this.

Help

Contacts Us

If for some reason you can not resolve your particular situation, don't hesitate to contact us. Fill in the form including your serial number and invoice number. You should expect a response within 24 hours.

Returns

If you are unsatisfied with your Upson product, you can request a return within 30 days of original purchase. Fill in the form and once the return has been recieved and authorized, you will recieve a mailing sticker to return your Upson product.
